Text Summarizer

How the App Works

This app uses supervised machine learning to summarize long pieces of text. It is powered by the BART which is Bidirectional and Auto-Regressive Transformers model, which has been trained on a large amount of text data. The app takes the user input and produces a summary based on the previous patterns from the data.

Enter your text, and the app will return a summary to your prompt. For the best results, try entering text between 500 and 1,000 words. Thanks! - Zain
